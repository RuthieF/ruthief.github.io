Fitt lets you connect and create fitness challenges no matter you or your friends tracking devices.

What Is Fitt?

Social Connection For All
It doesn’t matter what device* or phone you have, Fitt connects you with your friends. Instantly find Facebook, Jawbone UP and Fitbit friends so you can keep each other moving.

Create Challenges
Motivate your Fitbit and Jawbone UP friends to move with you. It doesnt metter your pace or fitness level, Fitt tracks your overall steps.

Why Challenges?
We move a little more when there’s an incentive (carrot dangling). Challenges motivate you to compete with friends for bragging right or perhaps a wager like dinner or the yard work.
When you complete a Challenge you also earn Rewards Points, save them up for cool prizes.
Fitt is a challenge platform tracked in steps, designed to motivate you to move and be active.
